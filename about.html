Our Story

Redefining Real Estate Since 2011

Aura Realty was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform the real estate experience from a stressful transaction into an exciting journey. Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, saw an industry ripe for innovation — one where technology could enhance, not replace, the human touch.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a boutique agency in Miami to a national brand with offices in 12 cities. Our success is built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to exceeding our clients' expectations.

Today, Aura Realty is recognized as one of the nation's premier real estate firms, consistently ranked among the top 1% of brokerages nationwide. We've helped over 8,000 families find their dream homes and facilitated billions of dollars in transactions.
